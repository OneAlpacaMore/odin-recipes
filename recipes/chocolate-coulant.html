Chocolate coulant

What is Cholate Coulant?

Chocolate coulant, the ideal dessert for chocolate addicts. The chocolate coulant It is a delicious dessert of French origin, very simple to prepare and a dessert with which we can surprise our guests. They also call it chocolate volcano, because when this cake is broken, the melted chocolate comes out and it looks like a volcano.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 100 gr. sugar
  • 40 gr. Of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 200 gr. chocolate for desserts
  • 80 gr. of butter

Directions

  1. To prepare the chocolate coulant, we will start by putting the oven to heat to 180ºC, heat up and down.
  2. In a bowl we put the butter and chocolate, in another the eggs and sugar.
  3. We put the chocolate and butter in the microwave or we can melt it in a bain-marie, on the other hand we beat the sugar and eggs with the rods.
  4. We add the sifted flour and mix little by little.
  5. In this mixture we will put the chocolate that we have melted together with the butter and cocoa powder.
  6. Mix with a spatula with enveloping movements, little by little until all the chocolate is well mixed.
  7. We prepare some molds and spread them with a little butter.
  8. We fill the molds filling them ¾ parts of their capacity, as they rise.
  9. We put the molds in the oven for 7 minutes, it will vary according to the oven, but to guide myself I look at the upper part of the coulant, when you see them without the chocolate shine they are ready. You have to be very aware of them.
  10. Once they are, you remove them from the oven, we remove them from the molds very carefully and serve hot.
  11. And ready to eat
