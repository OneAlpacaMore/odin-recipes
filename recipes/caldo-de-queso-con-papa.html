Caldo de queso con papa

What is Caldo de Queso con Papa?

Mexican Caldo de Queso con Papa is a soup from the northern states in Mexico, a traditonal dish from the State of Sonora. It's a delicious & healthy soup with chunks of potatoes and queso fresco, what is curd cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilo potatoes peeled and cut into squares
  • 1/2 kilo queso fresco
  • 1 tablespoon bouillon granules
  • 1 garlic
  • 1/2 chopped onion
  • 1 red tomato or chopped tomato
  • 1/2 chopped white onion
  • 2 poblano chiles
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 inches oregano
  • pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of the oil in a medium or large pot. Add the onions and saute over medium heat about 5 minutes. After that, add the garlic and cook for one minute.
  2. Add the tomato nad cook for 5 minutes or until they are soft.
  3. Add the potatoes, salt, pepper and oregano. And mix well and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
  4. Now add water, and the chicken bouillon. Cover the pot with a lid and cook until the potatoes are cooked through for about 25 to 30 mins.
  5. When the potatoes are soft, add the chile strips and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Finally, add the milk (warm, so that it doesn't curd when added to the hot broth)
